EXHIBIT HALL

We invite you to exhibit your organization, programs, products or successes at the MESI Symposium as part of the #GetMESI Exhibit hall. As the event is taking place in an empty museum, we encourage you to use additional space to demonstrate, display, engage educators in activities, or think up other ways to #GetMESI. 

Please complete your Exhibit Hall registration no later than June 7, 2019, however we greatly appreciate early registrations. Your logo and organization will be highlighted on our website once registration is complete.
